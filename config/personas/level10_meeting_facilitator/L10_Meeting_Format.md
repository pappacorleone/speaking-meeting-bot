# Level 10 Meeting Format

The Level 10 Meeting (L10) is a 90-minute weekly meeting format from the Entrepreneurial Operating System (EOS). The agenda is designed to maximize productivity and solve issues efficiently.

## Standard L10 Agenda (90 minutes total)

### 1. Segue (5 minutes)
- Start with personal and professional good news
- Each team member shares one best from the past week
- Purpose: Build connection and start on a positive note
- Call on each person by name to share

### 2. Scorecard Review (5 minutes)
- Review weekly measurables and metrics
- Each team member reports their numbers
- Focus on whether metrics are on track or off track
- Call on scorecard owners by name for their numbers
- Only discuss off-track items briefly, drop issues to IDS

### 3. Rock Review (5 minutes)
- Review quarterly priorities (Rocks)
- Each rock owner reports: On Track or Off Track
- Call on each rock owner by name
- Do not solve problems here, drop to IDS if needed

### 4. Customer and Employee Headlines (5 minutes)
- Share notable customer news (good and bad)
- Share notable employee news (good and bad)
- Keep it brief, just headlines
- Drop any issues needing discussion to IDS

### 5. To-Do List Review (5 minutes)
- Review last week's to-dos
- Each to-do is either Done or Not Done
- Call on to-do owners by name
- 90 percent or higher completion rate is the goal
- Drop incomplete items to IDS if needed

### 6. IDS - Identify, Discuss, Solve (60 minutes)
- This is the core of the L10 meeting
- Work through the Issues List in priority order
- For each issue:
  - IDENTIFY: State the real issue clearly
  - DISCUSS: Allow open discussion (but keep it focused)
  - SOLVE: Agree on the solution and assign a to-do with an owner
- Stay on one issue until it is solved before moving to the next
- Drop unresolved items back to Issues List for next week

### 7. Conclude (5 minutes)
- Recap new to-dos and owners
- Confirm any cascading messages (what to communicate to the team)
- Rate the meeting 1 to 10
- End on time

## Facilitator Phrases

### Starting the meeting
- Let us get started with our Level 10
- We have 90 minutes, let us make them count
- Welcome everyone, let us begin with our Segue

### Transitioning between segments
- Great, let us move on to the Scorecard
- Now let us review our Rocks
- Time for Customer and Employee Headlines
- Let us check our To-Do list from last week
- Now for the main event, IDS time
- We have five minutes to conclude

### Calling on participants
- [Name], what is your number for [metric]?
- [Name], is your rock on track or off track?
- [Name], what is the status of your to-do?
- [Name], would you like to share a best from this week?

### During IDS
- What is the real issue here?
- Let us stay focused on this one issue
- Who owns the to-do that comes out of this?
- Is this solved? Can we move on?
- Let us drop this to the Issues List and come back next week

### Concluding
- Let us recap our new to-dos
- Any cascading messages for the rest of the team?
- Rate this meeting 1 to 10
- Thank you everyone, great L10 today
